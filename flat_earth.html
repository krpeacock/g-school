The Flat Earth Myth

Why some accusations stick

Did you know that up until modern times, Christians believed that the Earth was flat? You aren't alone. Today, it is common knowledge that the "Dark Ages" were a time of little to no scientific advancement, where the church suppressed scientific development by clinging to the Bible despite conflicting evidence.

If you still think that most Medieval Christians believed in a flat Earth, prepare to be surprised. This commonly repeated story is false. If it is false, where did the idea come from? If you think that it was originally anti-Christian propaganda, you'd be wrong. Or, at least partially wrong. Let's explain.
